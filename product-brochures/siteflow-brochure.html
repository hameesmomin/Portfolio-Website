How The Workflow Works

The customer can start in standalone mode with manual records and demo data, then add integrations when the business is ready. Integrations enhance the workflow but are not required for daily use.

  1. Project is created with contractor, client, budget, milestone, and team information.
  2. Site engineer submits daily report with photos, progress, safety, and material updates.
  3. Snags, issues, approvals, and material requests are assigned to responsible parties.
  4. AI produces a daily project summary and flags delay or safety risks.
  5. Managers review budget variance, timeline variance, and contractor rankings.
  6. Client-ready report is exported or scheduled for stakeholders.
