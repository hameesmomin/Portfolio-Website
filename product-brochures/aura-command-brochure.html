How The Workflow Works

The customer can start in standalone mode with manual records and demo data, then add integrations when the business is ready. Integrations enhance the workflow but are not required for daily use.

  1. Lead is created from WhatsApp, website form, CRM, API, or manual entry.
  2. Aura assigns an agent based on rules, capacity, source, or manager choice.
  3. SLA timer starts and the shared inbox keeps the conversation visible.
  4. AI summarizes the customer need and suggests a reply or next best action.
  5. Follow-up task is created automatically when the lead needs a future touch.
  6. Manager reviews pipeline health, conversion, missed opportunities, and revenue forecast.
