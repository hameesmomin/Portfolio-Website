How The Workflow Works

The customer can start in standalone mode with manual records and demo data, then add integrations when the business is ready. Integrations enhance the workflow but are not required for daily use.

  1. User uploads contract, license, invoice, ID, or compliance document.
  2. Documind classifies the file and prepares OCR/extraction metadata.
  3. AI summarizes the document and highlights important fields or risks.
  4. Reviewer approves, requests changes, archives, or assigns owner.
  5. Renewal reminders and document timelines keep the record alive.
  6. Executives review compliance score, backlog, missing documents, and renewal forecast.
