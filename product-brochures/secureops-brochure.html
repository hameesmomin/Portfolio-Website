How The Workflow Works

The customer can start in standalone mode with manual records and demo data, then add integrations when the business is ready. Integrations enhance the workflow but are not required for daily use.

  1. Security alert, risk, vulnerability, or compliance gap is logged.
  2. SecureOps assigns severity, owner, due date, and remediation status.
  3. Evidence and activity are captured in the audit trail.
  4. AI assistant summarizes the risk and suggests remediation tasks.
  5. Management reviews posture score, incident trends, compliance status, and open critical items.
  6. Executive report is exported or scheduled for leadership review.
